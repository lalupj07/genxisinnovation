GenXis Innovations — Privacy-First, Local-First Software Lab, Kerala India

GenXis Innovations is a privacy-first software lab based in Kerala, India. We build local-first desktop software where your data stays on your device — never in the cloud.

Our Products

GenXBill — Free, open-source GST billing and inventory management for Windows. Unlimited invoices, smart invoicing, HR management, 100% offline local storage.

FamBudget — Privacy-focused family finance manager. Visual budgeting with donut charts, goal tracking, multi-currency support, 100% local storage.

GenXLink — Cross-platform P2P remote desktop built in Rust. End-to-end encrypted, 60fps screen share, advanced NAT traversal. No relay servers.

NeuralCore — Edge AI runtime for running LLMs locally on device. No cloud, no latency, fully private on-device AI inference.
