GenXis Innovations – Secure & High-Performance Software Lab in India

GenXis Innovations is a privacy-first, high-performance software lab based in Kerala, India. We build secure, local-first digital solutions for individuals and businesses — products and services where your data stays on your device, not the cloud.

Our Products

GenXBill – The ultimate open-source billing & inventory management suite for Windows. Smart invoicing, real-time inventory tracking, HR management, and business intelligence reports, all powered by Flutter with offline-first local storage.

FamBudget – A powerful, privacy-focused desktop application for family finance management. Features visual budgeting with donut charts, financial goal tracking, multi-currency support, and 100% local storage — your data never leaves your device.

GenXLink – Cross-platform, lightweight remote desktop optimized for low latency and privacy. Written in Rust for maximum performance. End-to-End encrypted with advanced NAT traversal.

NeuralCore – Next-generation local AI processing unit designed for edge devices. Brings Large Language Model inference directly to edge devices without cloud connectivity.

Our Services

Custom Engineering – From low-level system drivers to high-scale web platforms. We build software in Rust, C++, and modern web frameworks that is robust, maintainable, and tailored to your specific needs.
